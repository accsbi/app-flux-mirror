### [ App Description ] {Google / APP / WEB}

**Classic Simple Casino War** is an easy card game you can play on your own.

You and the dealer each show one card, and the higher card wins. That's it — anyone can pick it up right away.

No sign-up and no online play. Just a quick, relaxing card game with a classic, vintage design. Cards are dealt at random.

### [ App Features ] {Google / WEB}

* Classic card-room look: gold frames and a calm, vintage table.
* Simple rules and easy one-hand play — great for small screens.
* Light and fast. Made to run smoothly even on older Android phones.

### [ Quick Start ] {APP / WEB}

* Set your BET and start the round.
* You and the dealer each turn over one card.
* The higher card wins, and your COIN goes up or down.
* If it's a tie, you can choose to go to War: one more card decides it.

### [ How to Play ] {APP / WEB}

#### Steps

1. Set your BET and start the round.
2. You and the dealer each reveal one card.
3. The higher card wins the round.
4. Your COIN goes up or down based on the result.

#### Ties

* If both cards are the same, it's a tie.
* You can go to "War": one more card from each side decides the winner.

* To quit, tap HOME at the top left.
